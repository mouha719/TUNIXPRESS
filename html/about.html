About Us

Ayoub Sliti, Saiffedine Hmaied, and Mohamed Nour Mesbehi are ambitious students at the National School of Computer Science (ENSI) who have developed **TUNIXPRESS**, an e-commerce website designed to enhance the online shopping experience in Tunisia. Combining their technical expertise and entrepreneurial mindset, they built a modern, user-friendly platform that allows users to browse, purchase, and manage products with ease. TUNIXPRESS reflects their ability to turn innovative ideas into practical digital solutions , and it stands as a testament to their teamwork, creativity, and passion for web development.
